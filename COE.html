The Centre of Excellence for Advanced Computing provides a broad range of industry-relevant in-demand certifications from leading companies in the world and also supports the computational research & development needs of businesses. The CoE & CoC’s role is to enable and empower students with Industry 4.0 and beyond skills, do real-time projects, and get ready for the tech-driven market of 2030 & onward through certifications from Apple, Microsoft, Adobe, IBM, and many more.
SKLZ TECT team provides expertise & training on high-performance computing devices for cloud computing, application programming & optimization, data management, database development, data analytics, web application development, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning applications, iOS, Cyber Security.
